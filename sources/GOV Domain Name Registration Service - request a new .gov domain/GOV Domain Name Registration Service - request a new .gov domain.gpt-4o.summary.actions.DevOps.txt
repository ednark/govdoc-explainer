1. **Obtain a .gov Domain**:
   - Ensure your government web application uses a .gov domain to establish trust and compliance.

2. **Check Domain Availability**:
   - Search and verify that the desired .gov domain is available and compliant with naming conventions.

3. **Domain Management**:
   - Ensure proper procedures for managing the .gov domain, including DNS configurations, SSL certificates, and regular domain validations.

4. **Security Updates**:
   - Implement and regularly update security measures strictly adhering to government standards.

5. **Backup and Recovery**:
   - Develop and maintain a backup and disaster recovery plan for the .gov domain infrastructure.

6. **Monitoring and Logging**:
   - Establish continuous monitoring and comprehensive logging for the .gov domain to detect and respond to any security incidents promptly.
