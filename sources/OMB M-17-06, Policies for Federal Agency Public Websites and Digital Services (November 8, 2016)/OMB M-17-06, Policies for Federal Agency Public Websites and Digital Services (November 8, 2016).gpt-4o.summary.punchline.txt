**Purpose of the Government Standards Document:**
Ensure federal websites are effective, user-friendly, and accessible to everyone.

**Punchline Summaries:**

- **DevOps Technician:** Set up and maintain secure, compliant infrastructure for federal websites with tools for analytics and feedback.
  
- **Designer:** Create user-friendly, accessible, and responsive designs for federal websites on all devices.

- **Developer:** Implement backend logic and ensure Drupal-powered federal websites meet security, accessibility, and usability standards.

- **Project Manager:** Oversee and coordinate tasks to ensure federal websites are user-centric, compliant, and deliver a seamless digital experience.
