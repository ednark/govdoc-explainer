### Government Standards Document Summary

**Primary Standards, Guidelines, and Requirements:**
1. **Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995**
   - **Key Requirement:** OMB approval is needed for most types of information collection from the public.

2. **Related Resources for PRA:**
   - **A Guide to the Paperwork Reduction Act (June 2019)**
   - **Federal Collection of Information**
   - **Improving Implementation of the Paperwork Reduction Act**
   - **OMB M-11-26:** Fast-Track Process for Collecting Service Delivery Feedback
   - **Executive Order 13571:** Streamlining Service Delivery and Improving Customer Service (April 2011)
   - **Social Media, Web-Based Interactive Technologies, and the Paperwork Reduction Act (April 2010)**
   
3. **Government Paperwork Elimination Act (GPEA)**
   - **Key Requirement:** Reduce or eliminate paperwork for public interactions with the government.

4. **Small Business Paperwork Relief Act of 2002**
   - **Key Requirement:** Provide relief to small businesses from paperwork burdens.

**Focused Sections:**

1. **Security:**
   - **Executive Order 13571:** Streamline service delivery, includes enhancing security procedures.

2. **Accessibility:**
   - **Government Paperwork Elimination Act (GPEA):** Aims to make government forms more accessible by reducing paperwork.

3. **User Experience:**
   - **OMB M-11-26:** Fast-track process to collect feedback on service delivery.
   - **Executive Order 13571:** Improving customer service.

4. **Compliance:**
   - **Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995:** Must get OMB approval before collecting information from the public.
   - **Small Business Paperwork Relief Act of 2002:** Ensures compliance easing for small businesses.
