**Summary of Government Standards Document**

**Primary Standards and Guidelines:**
1. **Training for Contractors**:
    - Authorized by Section 701 of the Foreign Service Act of 1980 (22 U.S.C. 4021).
    - Applies to U.S. citizens under contract, excluding personal services contractors (PSCs).

2. **Training Policy**:
    - Training for contractors is space-available and reimbursable.
    - No tuition for contractors when training on proprietary systems benefits the Department.
    - Training requests must be approved by the contracting officer or training officer.

**Sections Focusing on Key Areas**:
1. **Security**:
    - Not directly mentioned in the provided document sections.

2. **Accessibility**:
    - Not directly mentioned in the provided document sections.

3. **User Experience**:
    - Training availability on a space-available basis.
    - Tuition waivers for necessary system training.

4. **Compliance**:
    - Training authorized by the Foreign Service Act.
    - Approval needed from appropriate officers for training.

**Short Result**:
1. **Training**: Authorized by Foreign Service Act; applies to contractors.
2. **Policy**: Training is space-available and reimbursable; tuition-free for essential system training.
3. **Approval**: Contracting or training officers must approve training.
