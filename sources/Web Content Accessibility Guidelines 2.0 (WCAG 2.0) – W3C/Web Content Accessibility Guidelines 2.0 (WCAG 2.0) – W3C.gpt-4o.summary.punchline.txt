**Overall Single Sentence Purpose:**
This document provides guidelines for making web content accessible to people with disabilities, ensuring it is usable by a wider audience.

**Perspectives:**

- **DevOps Technician:**
  "Ensure the infrastructure supports accessible and compliant web applications that everyone can use."

- **Designer:**
  "Create user-friendly interfaces that are accessible and inclusive for people with various disabilities."

- **Developer:**
  "Implement backend systems that conform to accessibility standards, ensuring all users have equal access to content."

- **Project Manager:**
  "Organize and guide the project to meet accessibility standards, making sure the final product is usable by everyone, including those with disabilities."
