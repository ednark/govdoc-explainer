### Purpose Statement:
Ensure all IT activities in the Department follow the required standards, policies, and security measures.

### Summaries for Specific Roles:

- **DevOps Technician:** "Make sure our app's infrastructure meets all government IT policies and security standards."
  
- **Designer:** "Ensure the application's design is accessible and follows Section 508 accessibility standards."
  
- **Developer:** "Implement the backend in compliance with required IT, security, and data management standards."
  
- **Project Manager:** "Guide the team to follow government IT standards and ensure compliance in all project aspects."
