Certainly! Here’s a minimal list of actions that a project manager should track and organize to ensure compliance with the key aspects of Circular No. A-130 for a web application project:

1. **Data Protection**
   - Implement and document security measures to protect sensitive information.

2. **Privacy Impact Assessment**
   - Conduct and document Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs).

3. **Information Quality**
   - Ensure that the information provided is accurate, relevant, timely, and complete.

4. **Accessibility**
   - Guarantee the application meets Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

5. **Records Management**
   - Establish procedures for proper records management and archiving.

6. **Incident Response**
   - Develop and test incident response plans for data breaches or cyber incidents.

7. **Coordination with Stakeholders**
   - Maintain regular communication with stakeholders to ensure alignment on requirements and compliance.

8. **Documentation**
   - Keep a detailed record of compliance efforts, audits, and reviews.

9. **Risk Management**
   - Identify, assess, and mitigate risks related to information management.

10. **Continuous Monitoring**
    - Implement a plan for continuous monitoring and improvement of the application’s security and compliance posture.
