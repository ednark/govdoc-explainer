Certainly, for a Project Manager organizing work for a web application to show compliance with the E-Government Act of 2002, here's a minimal list of actions:

### Planning Phase
1. **Define Objectives:**
   - Ensure the project aligns with the mission and objectives specified by the government client.
  
2. **Client Communication:**
   - Establish clear communication channels for updates and feedback with the client.

### Development Phase
3. **User Access:**
   - Ensure public access to government information and services is easy and secure.
  
4. **Interoperability:**
   - Use technologies that ensure data and systems can interoperate with other government systems.
  
5. **Accessibility:**
   - Implement accessibility standards to make the application usable for people with disabilities.

6. **Privacy:**
   - Ensure compliance with privacy standards and protection of personal information.

7. **Information Security:**
   - Incorporate robust security measures to protect data integrity, confidentiality, and availability.

8. **Interagency Collaboration:**
   - If applicable, ensure the application supports interagency collaboration as required by the standards.

### Post-Development Phase
9. **Testing:**
   - Conduct thorough testing to ensure all government standards are met, focusing on security, usability, and performance.

10. **Client Review:**
    - Schedule review sessions with the client to ensure all compliance aspects are satisfactorily addressed.

11. **Documentation:**
    - Maintain detailed documentation showing alignment with government requirements.

### Deployment Phase
12. **Approval:**
    - Obtain necessary approvals from the client and relevant oversight bodies before deploying the application.

13. **Training & Support:**
    - Organize training sessions for client staff and provide ongoing support.

### Monitoring Phase
14. **Compliance Reports:**
    - Regularly generate compliance reports for client review and are prepared to make updates as regulations evolve.

15. **Continuous Monitoring:**
    - Implement tools for continuous monitoring to ensure ongoing compliance with government standards.

By tracking these actions, the Project Manager can ensure that the web application project remains compliant with government standards throughout its lifecycle.
