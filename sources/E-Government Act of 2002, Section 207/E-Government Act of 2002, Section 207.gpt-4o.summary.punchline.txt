**Overall Purpose:**
To make government services and information more accessible and efficient through the use of internet technology and improved data management.

**For a DevOps Technician:**
Ensure infrastructure is secure and robust to handle online government services and databases efficiently.

**For a Designer:**
Create user-friendly and accessible interfaces for public interaction with government services.

**For a Developer:**
Implement secure and reliable backend systems using Drupal to support government services online.

**For a Project Manager:**
Coordinate and track progress to ensure the successful completion of tasks that facilitate better digital government services.
