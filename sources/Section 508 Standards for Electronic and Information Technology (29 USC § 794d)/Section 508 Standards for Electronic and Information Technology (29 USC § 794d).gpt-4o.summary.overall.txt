### Government Standards Document Summary

#### Primary Standards, Guidelines, and Requirements

1. **Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973**
   - Requires federal agencies to make ICT accessible to people with disabilities.

2. **U.S. Access Board Final Rule (2017)**
   - Updated Section 508 standards and Section 255 guidelines for ICT; aligns with WCAG 2.0.

3. **FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation) Updates (2021)**
   - Key parts ensuring ICT accessibility in federal procurement.

4. **Related Laws**
   - Sections 501, 503, 504, 505 of the Rehabilitation Act.
   - Section 255 of the Communications Act.
   - ADA, Assistive Technology Act, Help America Vote Act, 21st Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act, 21st Century IDEA.

5. **Executive Orders and OMB Memos**
   - Related to diversity, equity, inclusion, accessibility, and the digital-first public experience.

6. **Agency Policies**
   - Must include and update accessibility policies to align with Section 508 requirements.

#### Security, Accessibility, User Experience, and Compliance

1. **Security**
   - **FAR 11.002(f)**
     - Document security needs and exemptions for accessibility.

2. **Accessibility**
   - **Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act**
     - Make ICT accessible to people with disabilities.
   - **U.S. Access Board Final Rule (2017)**
     - Updated standards aligning with WCAG 2.0.
   - **Related Laws (Sections 501, 503, 504, 505)**
     - Address discrimination and reasonable accommodation.

3. **User Experience**
   - **21st Century IDEA**
     - Modernize websites and digital services.
   - **Executive Orders and OMB Memos**
     - Improving accessibility and customer service.

4. **Compliance**
   - **FAR 39.2**
     - Ensure ICT accessibility in procurement.
   - **Agency Policies**
     - Regularly review and update to maintain compliance with Section 508.
