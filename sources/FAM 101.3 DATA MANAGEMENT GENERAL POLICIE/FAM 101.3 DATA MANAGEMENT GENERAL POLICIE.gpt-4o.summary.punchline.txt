**Overall Purpose:**
Ensure government data is managed and shared effectively while maintaining security and compliance with legal requirements.

**Punchline Summaries:**

- **DevOps Technician:**
You need to design and manage infrastructure to ensure data is secure, shareable, and compliant with legal standards.

- **Designer (Human Centered Design):**
Design the user experience to promote transparency and easy access to public data while ensuring compliance with privacy and security rules.

- **Developer (Drupal):**
Implement backend structures that facilitate data sharing and adhere to legal privacy and retention guidelines.

- **Project Manager:**
Coordinate tasks to ensure data management practices align with government policies and security requirements, ensuring project compliance and approval.
