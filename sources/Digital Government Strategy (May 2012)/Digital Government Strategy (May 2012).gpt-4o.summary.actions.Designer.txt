1. **Conduct User Research:**
   - Understand the needs, behaviors, and pain points of the target audience.
   
2. **Design with Mobile-First Approach:**
   - Ensure the application is fully functional and optimized for mobile devices.
   
3. **Implement Responsive Web Design:**
   - Use flexible grids, fluid images, and media queries to ensure the application works across various screen sizes and devices.
   
4. **Use Plain Language:**
   - Ensure all content is clear, concise, and easily understandable.
   
5. **Ensure Accessibility Compliance:**
   - Follow WAI-ARIA guidelines and Section 508 standards to make the application accessible to all users.
   
6. **Conduct Usability Testing:**
   - Test the application with real users to identify and fix usability issues.
   
7. **Apply Consistent Branding:**
   - Use a cohesive design language, including colors, fonts, and visuals, to maintain a consistent user experience.
   
8. **Implement Feedback Mechanisms:**
   - Include features that allow users to provide feedback easily.

9. **Optimize for Performance:**
   - Ensure fast load times and efficient use of resources to enhance user experience.
   
10. **Use Metadata and Structured Data:**
    - Employ structured data and metadata to improve search engine optimization and data retrieval.
