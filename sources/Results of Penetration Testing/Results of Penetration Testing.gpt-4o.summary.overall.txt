# Summary of FedRAMP Penetration Test Guidance

### Primary Standards, Guidelines, and Requirements:
1. **NIST SP 800-115**: Technical Guide to Information Security Testing and Assessment.
2. **NIST SP 800-145**: The NIST Definition of Cloud Computing.
3. **NIST SP 800-53**: Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations.
4. **NIST SP 800-53A**: Assessing Security and Privacy Controls in Federal Information Systems and Organizations.

### Key Sections:
1. **Scope of Testing**:
   - List of mandatory guidelines to test cloud services (SaaS, PaaS, IaaS).
2. **Threats**:
   - Identification of common threat models and attack models.
3. **Attack Vectors**:
   - Specific mandatory attack vectors for security testing.
4. **Scoping the Penetration Test**:
   - Defining boundaries and legal constraints for testing.
5. **Rules of Engagement (ROE)**:
   - Constraints and methodologies for carrying out penetration tests.

### Specific Areas:
1. **Security**:
   - Guidelines and detailed methodologies to ensure the system security through penetration testing.
2. **Compliance**:
   - Required use of authorized third-party assessment organizations (3PAOs).
3. **User Experience**:
   - Measures of attack impacts like phishing on user compliance and awareness.
4. **Accessibility**:
   - Categorization of cloud services with specific guidelines to support user-friendly practices.
