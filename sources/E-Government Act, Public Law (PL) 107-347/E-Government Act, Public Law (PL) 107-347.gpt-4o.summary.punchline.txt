### Overall Purpose
The document establishes standards for using Internet technology to improve public access to government services and information.

### Perspectives

**DevOps Technician:**
"Ensure our infrastructure is secure, scalable, and compliant with federal guidelines to support seamless citizen access."

**Designer:**
"Design user-friendly, accessible, and privacy-compliant interfaces to enhance public interaction with government services online."

**Developer:**
"Implement backend logic in Drupal to meet federal standards for security, data handling, and efficient service delivery."

**Project Manager:**
"Organize and guide the team in meeting federal standards to create secure, accessible, and citizen-friendly web applications."
